About Me

Hi! I am Daniil Yurshevich and I am passionate about math, technology, and evidence-based knowledge. I am currently studying maths at EPFL, and I'm mostly into algebraic things like algebraic topology, algebraic geometry, and homotopical algebra, which are probably the most appealing to me at the moment. I am also a huge enthusiast of applied math, particularly machine learning. Especially the topic of AI safety and alignment resonates with me deeply. I am a member of the Safe AI Lausanne community. I am very excited about interpretability and actively learning about it. You can check out my GitHub repository where I am collaborating with Nikita Khomich to regularly publish our research in the field of interpretability. Besides the code I am planning to write some blog posts about the topic, so stay tuned!
